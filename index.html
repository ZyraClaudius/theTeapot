What is The Teapot?

A Place for Great Theatre

Do you like being entertained? Laughing? Crying your eyes out? Maybe not so much the last one, but imagine it's due to the goodness of art! Doesn't sound so bad now, does it? Then come on down, we'll put the kettle on...

A Place for Comforting Pasta Dishes

Picture this: it's been a stressful morning at work. You're feeling hard done by. If only there were somewhere to get a quality pasta-based meal at a reasonable price, and leave another one hanging for someone in need! Well, such a place exists, and there's a cuppa waiting to wash it all down.

A Community

We love to get involved with community theatre projects, and our "hanging pasta dish" scheme is helping to keep people fed - because we believe everyone deserves a bowl of pasta and a cuppa at the end of a long day.
